Our Story

Haworth Men's Shed was established to provide a friendly, inclusive environment for men to come together. We recognize that men often find it easier to talk when they are working "shoulder to shoulder" on a project.

Whether you are a master craftsman or have never held a hammer in your life, there is a place for you here. We focus on mental health, community service, and preserving traditional skills.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: Everyone is welcome, regardless of skill level.
  • Well-being: Fighting isolation through friendship.
  • Sustainability: Repairing and upcycling items for the community.
