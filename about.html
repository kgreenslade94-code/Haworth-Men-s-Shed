About the Shed

Our Purpose

The Haworth Men’s Shed is more than just a workshop; it’s a place for connection. We provide a space where men can come together to work on projects, share skills, and most importantly, support one another.

Men’s Sheds are vital community hubs that help combat social isolation by providing a meaningful way to spend time. Whether you’re an expert craftsman or have never held a hammer in your life, you are welcome here.

Skill Sharing

Learn everything from woodworking to electronics repair from fellow members.

Wellbeing

A safe space to talk, listen, and belong to a supportive community.

Who Can Join?

Our doors are open to men from all walks of life in the Haworth area. If you enjoy making things, fixing things, or just having a chat over a cup of tea, the Shed is for you.
