Our Mission

Haworth Men's Shed is a community-run space where men can meet to share skills, work on projects, or simply enjoy a cup of tea and a chat.

Our primary aim is to promote social wellbeing and reduce isolation through the power of practical creativity and companionship.

Support the Shed

As a registered charity, we rely on the generosity of our community to keep our workshop running and our tools sharp.

What We Do

  • Woodworking: From birdhouses to furniture repair.
  • Gardening: Managing our community allotment.
  • Metalwork: Basic repairs and creative projects.
  • Tech & Repair: Electronics and small appliance fixes.
  • Socializing: Regular trips, talks, and daily coffee.

Who Can Join?

We are open to all men in the Worth Valley area. No previous experience is needed—just a willingness to get involved!
