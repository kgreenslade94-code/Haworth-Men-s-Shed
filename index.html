What is a Men's Shed?

Haworth Men's Shed is a community space where men can connect, create, and converse. We provide the tools and the space to work on projects at your own pace, in a friendly and inclusive environment.

Whether you are a master craftsman or just looking for a chat and a project to tinker with, there is a place for you here.

Activities & Projects

  • Woodworking and Furniture Repair
  • Community Gardening Projects
  • Metalwork and DIY
  • Restoration of local landmarks
  • Simply having a coffee and a catch-up
